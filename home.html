Satisfy your Matcha cravings!

Matcha is finely ground powder of specially grown and processed green tea.
The traditional Japanese tea ceremony centers on the preparation, serving, and drinking of matcha.
In modern times, matcha has also come to be used to flavour and dye foods such as mochi and soba noodles, green tea ice cream and a variety of wagashi (Japanese confectionery).
The former is often referred to as ceremonial-grade matcha, meaning that the matcha powder is good enough for tea ceremony. The latter is referred to as culinary-grade matcha.
In Cafe Aroma, Matcha is used to make your latte, flavour your ice cream and cake.
Whatever the use, Cafe Aroma and Matcha is a matcha made in heaven!

Cafe Aroma Offers 15 May, 2015
